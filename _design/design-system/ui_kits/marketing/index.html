▶ what it does

three features.
that's it.

01

save anything

a screenshot, a draft tweet, a regret. it goes in your void. nobody else sees it unless you say so.

02

publish if you mean it

flip a synth public and it gets a permalink. simple. no algorithm. no engagement metrics that aren't a single integer.

03

export when you leave

tar.gz of everything. one click. we don't trap you. we don't even like you that much.
